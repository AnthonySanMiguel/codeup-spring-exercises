Overview: A React tutorial for building a tic-tac-toe game with alternating player moves, an interactive "history" feature to allow rewinding of moves, and a status change showing either winner or draw for results.

Player is greeted by a game board consisting of 9 squares, prompting the first player ("X") to make the first move.

Players take turns clicking on one square in the game board, where an image will be placed on click denoting who made the selection. Additionally, the status screen at the top will show which player's turn it is, and the history log on the right will keep track of the movements.

Once the victory conditions/pattern is met, the game will end with a message announcing the winner, as well as highlighting where the conditions were fulfilled, and display their location on the game board.

By clicking any of the history log buttons, players may "rewind" their session to render the game to the respective moment in time where that particular move was made.
