Overview: A tutorial on how to recreate the arcade hit Pacman in Java. Credit to: http://zetcode.com/javagames/pacman/ for the tutorial.

Completing the tutorial will leave you with a convincing clone of this famous video game hit. Pacman is controlled by the arrow keys, and the ghosts will end the game if they catch him.

Of course, feel free to experiment once completed and change the colors, ghost pngs, and even design your own maze!
